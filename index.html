About me

Hi! I'm Chandan Yadav, Associate Lead – Field Operations & Analytics at Shadowfax. I manage inventory control, store compliance and field-engineer performance across a Pan-India store network, and build the MIS dashboards, pendency trackers and reconciliation tools that keep field teams and leadership looking at the same numbers.

I started out in web development, and that background still shows up in how I work — instead of relying only on spreadsheets, I build small internal tools and dashboards to track material movement, automate reconciliation, and turn raw operational data into something leadership can act on immediately. I'm continuously sharpening my skills in SQL, Python, Power BI and Tableau.
